Phaedra was the apprentice of Thayan Zulkir James Earthsea.

She was a tall and severe-looking woman; she might have been beautiful, but a hollowness behind the eyes, a perpetual sneer, and a gaunt face similar to her master’s gave her a pronounced, ghoulish affect.

Like many other Red Wizards, she completely lacked hair—even eyebrows and lashes—and what skin was visible outside her voluminous scarlet robes was covered in a twisting, shifting canvas of arcane tattoos.

heading

The party only directly encountered Phaedra once, while infiltrating the false Temple of Ao in the Tilver Valley. They came upon her and Earthsea working with a bizarre techno-magical apparatus containing an unstable portal to the Shadowfell.

Phaedra toyed with the party briefly before Kadarr drew the Black Blade, decapitating both Earthsea and 8U8A in his madness.

She seized up Earthsea’s head and bolted for a teleportation circle, closing the ethereal door behind her.

heading

Phaedra’s remains were later found in the Cloister of the Old Night in the Shadowfell. She had been violently torn apart—presumably a victim of the horribly mutated Averlyn.

While trying to speak with dead, Beldrin set off a devious countermeasure that had survived even her death: her skull burst out of her body, and the party barely had time to dive for cover before it exploded.

Descending into the labs beneath the ruined abbey, they discovered that she had brutally tortured both Arctus Mursk and the Gondola engineer Donal Maan, and killed Maan’s captive family.
